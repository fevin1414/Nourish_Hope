Login / Register

How It Works

1. Register or Login

Click Login / Register in the top bar. Fill in your details (name, email, password, role = “donor”, phone) and submit.

2. Navigate the Dashboard

Once logged in, the Donor Dashboard appears. Use the sidebar buttons to switch between:

  • Orphanages — View and select an orphanage.
  • My Donations — See all past food & money donations.
  • Requests — Track orphanage pickup requests and statuses.

3. Select an Orphanage

Click the Food or Money button next to an orphanage in the table. A donation form will slide down for you to enter details.

4. Make a Food Donation

In the form, enter:

  • Food Type
  • Quantity
  • Shelf Life (date picker)
  • Pickup Location

Click Donate. You’ll see a success alert and receive an SMS confirmation (if phone provided).

5. Make a Money Donation

Switch to the Money tab in the form, enter amount (₹) and optional message, then click Donate. You’ll get a confirmation alert and SMS.

6. View My Donations

Go to “My Donations” in the sidebar. You’ll see:

  • Food Donations — shows each quantity × foodType and recipient orphanage.
  • Money Donations — shows each ₹amount and recipient orphanage.

7. Track Requests & Status

Under “Requests,” orphanages will reserve or mark donations delivered. Status badges—available, reserved, delivered—show current state.

8. Logout

Click Logout at the top right to end your session securely.
